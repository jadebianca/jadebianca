Trusting Myself:
The Key To Building a Meaningful Career

By: Jade Bianca Portillo • January 21, 2026

Intro • Growing and Learning • Overcoming Challenges • Having Faith in Every Step

Elle Woods once said, You must always have faith in people. And most importantly, you must always have faith in yourself. That quote reminds me how important it is to trust myself when thinking about my career and future. To be honest, if someone asked me where would I see myself in the next five years, my mind would completly go blank. Maybe I’ll work as a web developer or a data engineer, but who knows? Life is full of surprises and focusing on the present is the best thing that I can do.

Growing and Learning Each Day

As an IT student, my main goal is to develop and improve my skills everyday. Since technology is constantly evolving, I am willing to adapt, learn new things, and grow along the way. I want to build something meaningful through my hard work and dedication. Instead of seeing mistakes as failures, I try to treat them as a lessons that will help me grow. Every small improvement matters and contributes to my progress. Currently, I focus on developing skills and confidence that will help me not only in school but also in the future. I want myself to look back and know that I gave my best and stayed true to myself.

Back to top ↑

Overcoming Doubts and Challenges

There have been times when school tested my confidence and doubted my abilities. I struggled, failed, and felt frustrated, but I never gave up. Over time, I learned to adjust and find ways to understand things on my own. Those experiences shaped how I approach my future. They taught me that patience and effort matter more than instant success. I’ve learned to trust that focusing on the present will naturally guide me to the right place. Looking back, I can see that every small step prepared me for bigger challenges.

Back to top

Having Faith in Every Step

Overall, I realize that trusting myself and staying focused on the present are the keys to building a meaningful career. The future isn’t something you can fully predict, but that’s what makes it exciting. All you can really do is give everything you’ve got in the moment and hope for the best. Having faith gives me confidence to explore opportunities, take risks, and keep improving. With this mindset, I feel ready to create a future that feels fulfilling and true to who I am.
